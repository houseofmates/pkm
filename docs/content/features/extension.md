# extension

the pkm browser extension lets you [[capture-system|capture]] web content into your knowledge system without leaving your browser.

## features

- clip full pages, selections, or highlights
- auto-extract metadata (title, url, date)
- send captures to your inbox or a specific [[database]]
- works in firefox and chromium browsers

the extension is the front door of the [[capture-workflow]]. see also: [[capture-system]]
