# ai co-pilot

the ai co-pilot is your writing and thinking assistant inside pkm. it lives in the sidebar and helps you work with your [[notes]].

## what it can do

- summarize long captures
- suggest [[tags]] and [[connections]]
- draft text from bullet points
- answer questions about your knowledge base (via [[oracle-search]])
- help with [[progressive-summarization]]

the co-pilot uses your local llm through [[wilson-rag]]. see also: [[ai-features]], [[llm-native]]
