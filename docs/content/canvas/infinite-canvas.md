# infinite canvas

the infinite canvas is a boundless 2d workspace where you can arrange [[notes]], [[embeds]], images, and [[blocks]] freely in space.

## why infinite canvas

- no page boundaries — ideas can sprawl as needed
- spatial arrangement adds meaning beyond text
- supports [[visual-thinking]] and [[moodboards]]
- zoom in for detail, zoom out for overview

the canvas is the foundation of [[canvas-system]] and [[collaborative-canvases]].
