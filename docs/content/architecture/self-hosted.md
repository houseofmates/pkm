# self-hosted

pkm is self-hosted — your data lives on your own server, not someone else's cloud.

## why self-host

- full ownership of your data
- no vendor lock-in or surprise shutdowns
- customize and extend without permission
- privacy by default

the stack runs with docker compose: vite [[frontend]], node [[backend]], nocobase, and [[n8n]] for [[automation]]. see also: [[system-overview]]
