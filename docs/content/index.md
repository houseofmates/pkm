## what is pkm?

pkm combines:

* infinite [[canvas system|canvas]] for visual thinking
* [[nocobase collections|nocobase]] with 12+ [[database views]]
* markdown [[notes]] with [[connections]]
* ai-powered [[ai features]] like wilson rag
* self-hosted [[web hosting]] on your domain

imagine a space where ideas flow between databases, canvases, and notes. where [[knowledge graphs]] emerge naturally from [[tagging]] and [[links]].
